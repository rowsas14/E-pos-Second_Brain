# Product Reviews

## Purpose
Customer product ratings and review moderation for storefront trust and merchandising feedback.

## Why this feature exists
Reviews are part of E-Commerce experience and require moderated tenant-owned content.

## Scope
- customer submits review
- moderator approves or rejects review
