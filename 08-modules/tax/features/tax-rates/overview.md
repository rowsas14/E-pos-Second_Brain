# Tax Rates

## Purpose
Tenant-owned effective-dated tax/VAT rate master used during pricing and transaction snapshot generation.

## Why this feature exists
Actual percentage rates may change over time and need history-aware application.

## Scope
- create tax rate
- expire and replace with new effective rate
