# POS Checkout

## Purpose
Barcode/search-driven cashier sale creation, pricing, totals, payment collection, and receipt issue.

## Why this feature exists
Fast touchscreen-first checkout is one of the most important business requirements in the BRD.

## Scope
- scan or search variant
- edit cart line quantity/discount
- collect payment and complete sale
