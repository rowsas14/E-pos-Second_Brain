# Hold & Recall Sales

## Purpose
Temporary suspension of a POS sale so the cashier can resume it later.

## Why this feature exists
Real store counters need hold/recall for interrupted checkout, customer indecision, or queue management.

## Scope
- hold current sale
- search held sale
- recall and continue checkout
