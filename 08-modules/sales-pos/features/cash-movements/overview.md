# Cash Movements

## Purpose
Non-sale cash events within a till session such as cash in, cash out, safe drop, and float add.

## Why this feature exists
Till reconciliation must include operational cash movement outside direct sale payments.

## Scope
- record cash in/out
- perform safe drop
- review cash events during register close
