# Daily Sales Reporting

## Purpose
Operational reporting for daily sales totals, outlet summaries, channel summaries, and payment mix visibility.

## Why this feature exists
The MVP explicitly requires daily sales and channel/outlet reporting for business operations.

## Scope
- view daily sales dashboard
- filter by outlet and channel
- export summary
