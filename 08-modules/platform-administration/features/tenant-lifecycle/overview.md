# Tenant Lifecycle

## Purpose
Activation, suspension, and operating-status control for tenant businesses after onboarding.

## Why this feature exists
The platform needs a controlled lifecycle for commercial operations, support, contract issues, and emergency isolation.

## Scope
- activate tenant after onboarding
- temporarily suspend tenant operations
- reactivate tenant after issue resolution
