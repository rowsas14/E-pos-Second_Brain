# Receipt Delivery

## Purpose
Delivery of generated receipts through thermal print, PDF, or email channels.

## Why this feature exists
Generation and delivery have different failure modes and integrations, so they should be documented separately.

## Scope
- print thermal receipt
- email receipt
- download PDF/A4 render
