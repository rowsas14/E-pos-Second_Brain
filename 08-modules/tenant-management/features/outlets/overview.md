# Outlets

## Purpose
Physical or logical operating locations such as store, warehouse, dark store, and fulfillment center.

## Why this feature exists
Unified commerce needs outlet-aware stock, POS execution, fulfillment assignment, and reporting.

## Scope
- create outlet
- assign outlet type and address
- activate/deactivate outlet for operations
