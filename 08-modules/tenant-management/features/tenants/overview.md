# Tenants

## Purpose
Tenant root business record that defines operational identity, locale, currency, timezone, and commerce mode.

## Why this feature exists
Tenant is the top ownership boundary for the unified platform. Most business and technical isolation rules depend on it.

## Scope
- view tenant profile
- update non-lifecycle tenant properties
- read tenant mode and locale during downstream setup
