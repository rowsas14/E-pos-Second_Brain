# Membership Tiers

## Purpose
Tenant-owned loyalty or membership tier definitions with points multiplier and status.

## Why this feature exists
The platform needs a tenant-specific membership foundation without assuming one global loyalty identity.

## Scope
- create tier
- change points multiplier or status
