# Discount Applications

## Purpose
Actual discount records applied to sale or order headers/lines, including direct and approved discounts.

## Why this feature exists
Approved intent and applied financial effect must be stored separately for auditability.

## Scope
- apply approved discount
- apply direct policy discount
- recompute affected totals
