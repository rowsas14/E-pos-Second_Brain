# Customer Auth

## Purpose
Tenant-aware login accounts and identities for customer sign-in using local or provider-based authentication.

## Why this feature exists
Online customers need account access, but auth identities must remain tenant-aware so one provider/email can exist per tenant separately.

## Scope
- register customer auth account
- log in with local or provider identity
- link or manage identity methods
