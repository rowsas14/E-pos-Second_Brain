# Stock Transfers

## Purpose
Controlled movement of stock between outlets or warehouse-type locations.

## Why this feature exists
Unified commerce needs stock repositioning between store and fulfillment locations without breaking auditability.

## Scope
- request transfer
- approve and ship transfer
- receive transfer and post final stock movement
