# Price Lists

## Purpose
Named price sets with channel, currency, date range, priority, and variant-level prices.

## Why this feature exists
Unified commerce needs controlled pricing that can differ by channel and time while still resolving from a shared catalog.

## Scope
- create price list
- add variant prices
- activate price list for effective period
